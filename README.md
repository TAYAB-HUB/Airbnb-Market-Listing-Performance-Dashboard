# Airbnb_Market_Listing_Performance_Dashboard
<h1>🏠 Airbnb Market &amp; Listing Performance Dashboard</h1>

<p>
  An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb’s listing growth,
  market share, accommodation pricing, city-level ratings, and customer review
  behavior across major global cities.
</p>

<h2>1. Short Description / Purpose</h2>

<p>
  The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Airbnb’s market performance
  across <strong>279,712 listings</strong>, <strong>182,024 hosts</strong>,
  and <strong>10 cities</strong>. It helps users understand historical growth,
  city competitiveness, pricing patterns, customer satisfaction, and seasonal
  travel trends.
</p>

<h2>2. Tech Stack</h2>

<p>The dashboard was developed using:</p>

<ul>
  <li>
    📊 <strong>Power BI Desktop</strong> – Dashboard development and data
    visualization.
  </li>
  <li>
    📂 <strong>Power Query</strong> – Data cleaning, transformation, and
    preparation.
  </li>
  <li>
    🧠 <strong>DAX</strong> – KPI calculations, percentages, cumulative
    measures, and dynamic analysis.
  </li>
  <li>
    🗂️ <strong>Data Modeling</strong> – Connecting listings, hosts, cities,
    property types, ratings, and reviews.
  </li>
  <li>
    📁 <strong>File Formats</strong> – <code>.pbix</code> for development and
    <code>.pdf</code>/<code>.png</code> for presentation.
  </li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Data Source</h2>

<p><strong>Source:</strong> Airbnb listings and reviews dataset.</p>

<p>The dataset includes:</p>

<ul>
  <li>279,712 listings</li>
  <li>182,024 hosts</li>
  <li>187,999 reviews</li>
  <li>144 property types</li>
  <li>10 major international cities</li>
  <li>Room types and average prices</li>
  <li>Host and Superhost information</li>
  <li>Customer ratings and review activity</li>
  <li>Listing creation dates and monthly review trends</li>
</ul>

<h2>4. Features / Highlights</h2>

<h3>Business Problem</h3>

<p>
  Airbnb operates across highly competitive tourism markets, making it
  difficult to quickly compare listing supply, pricing, customer satisfaction,
  and travel demand across cities.
</p>

<p>Important business questions include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Which cities have the largest Airbnb market share?</li>
  <li>How has Airbnb’s listing growth changed over time?</li>
  <li>Which property types command the highest prices?</li>
  <li>Which cities receive the strongest customer ratings?</li>
  <li>How frequently do customers leave reviews?</li>
  <li>How does review activity change throughout the year?</li>
</ul>

<h3>Goal of the Dashboard</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Monitor Airbnb’s historical listing growth.</li>
  <li>Compare market share across major cities.</li>
  <li>Analyze Superhost and non-Superhost listings.</li>
  <li>Compare average prices by room type.</li>
  <li>Evaluate customer ratings across important service dimensions.</li>
  <li>Identify review frequency and seasonal travel patterns.</li>
  <li>Support data-driven pricing, marketing, and expansion decisions.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Walkthrough of Key Visuals</h3>

<h4>📌 Key KPIs</h4>

<p>
  Displays <strong>279,712 listings</strong>,
  <strong>10 cities</strong>, <strong>182,024 hosts</strong>,
  <strong>144 property types</strong>, and
  <strong>187,999 reviews</strong>.
</p>

<h4>📈 New Listings Trend</h4>

<p>
  Tracks listing growth from 2008 to 2021 across private rooms, entire places,
  shared rooms, and hotel rooms. New listings peaked in 2015, slowed during
  2016–2017, recovered before 2020, and declined sharply during the COVID-19
  pandemic.
</p>

<h4>🌍 Market Share by City</h4>

<p>
  Compares Superhost and non-Superhost listings across cities. Paris, New York,
  and Sydney together represent almost half of total listings and
  <strong>48% of total reviews</strong>.
</p>

<h4>💰 Average Price by Room Type</h4>

<ul>
  <li>Hotel room: <strong>$800</strong></li>
  <li>Entire place: <strong>$673</strong></li>
  <li>Shared room: <strong>$580</strong></li>
  <li>Private room: <strong>$462</strong></li>
</ul>

<h4>⭐ City Ratings Matrix</h4>

<p>
  Compares accuracy, communication, cleanliness, location, and value ratings
  across cities. Mexico City records consistently strong ratings across the
  measured categories.
</p>

<h4>📝 Review Frequency Analysis</h4>

<p>
  Approximately <strong>98.8% of customers</strong> submitted three reviews or
  fewer. One unusual customer recorded 283 reviews, highlighting a possible
  data-quality issue or exceptional customer behavior.
</p>

<h4>📅 Monthly Review Distribution</h4>

<p>
  Paris and Rome dominate review activity from April to August, reflecting the
  European summer travel season. New York experiences stronger activity in
  November and December during the holiday season.
</p>

<h3>Business Impact &amp; Insights</h3>

<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>Market Prioritization:</strong> Identify cities with the strongest
    listing supply and customer activity.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Pricing Strategy:</strong> Compare room-type prices to support
    competitive pricing decisions.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Customer Experience:</strong> Identify service strengths and areas
    requiring improvement.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Seasonal Marketing:</strong> Plan campaigns around city-specific
    travel patterns.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Host Performance:</strong> Compare Superhost participation across
    markets.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Data Quality:</strong> Detect unusual review activity and potential
    anomalies.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Strategic Expansion:</strong> Discover markets with potential growth
    opportunities.
  </li>
</ul>
